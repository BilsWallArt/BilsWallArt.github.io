Bil's Wall Art
Wood Wall Art and Other Wood Stuff
Past and Present Work

I make each piece, using cedar or other soft wood which is cut, sanded, assembled and glued onto a hardboard panel. Some are painted and/or stained and may have purchased metal items added to the designs. All wood is final coated with a water-based polyurethane.

As with all wood creations wood grain, certain characteristics, thickness and coloring will vary from piece to piece. If you're interested in any of these items I will replicate, to the best of my ability, the pictured piece. We will also determine size, colors and/or other requests.
